GINGER

Ginger Mckenna, a hustler, and socialite married Ace under the condition that he will take care of her financially, despite her lack of feelings for him. When she can't stay away from her true love, she begins revaluating her marriage quickly realizing her mistake, leading her to abuse alcohol and drugs. She eventually runs away with the money she was promised but dies shortly after due to a drug overdose.

Lesson: Chasing money and materials is not the way to find true happiness.
